# Supported ITR Forms

## ITR-1 (SAHAJ)
For resident individuals with income up to ₹50 lakh
- Salary income
- One house property
- Other sources
- Agricultural income (up to ₹5,000)

## ITR-2
For individuals/HUF without business income
- Multiple income sources
- Capital gains
- Foreign income

## ITR-3
For individuals with business/profession income
- Business income
- Profession income
- Loss carry forward

## ITR-4 (SUGAM)
For presumptive income schemes
- Section 44AD
- Section 44ADA
- Section 44AE

## ITR-5
For partnership firms, LLPs, AOPs, BOIs

## ITR-6
For companies

## ITR-7
For trusts, political parties, institutions
